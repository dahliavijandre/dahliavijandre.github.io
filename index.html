Dahlia Vijandre

Art Historian

My name is Dahlia Louise Vijandre and I am currently enrolled at the University of California, Riverside. As a second year Art History student here at UCR, the curriculum and courses I've taken have deepened my fascination of art. My profession requires extensive hours of reading and examining art of different time periods, and how their iconographies distinguish themselves into certain eras of art.

My pursuit in Art History was not an abrupt decision I made when I was entering my first year of college. Although, my love for appreciating and creating art has always been apart of my long history of interests. The extracurricular activties I've partaken in during primary school always revolved around art. I was actively participating in art fairs, artwalks and my high school's graphic design club. Through all of these experiences, I discovered that the contextual history behind art plays a huge factor into the true meaning of an artpiece, foreshadowing my career.

As of right now, I balance my education of Art History with a part-time job at In-N-Out as a store associate. I actively participate at local art museums and events that invovle art. I am currently seeking internships that can provide me more experience as an Art Historian.
